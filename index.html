OUR ORGANISATION'S VISION AND MISSION

VISION

We aim to empower local institutions as the foundation for achieving sustainable development with special focus on women, children and physically challenged persons in Nigeria with Enugu State as the springboard

MISSION

Nigeria today is saddled with poverty, insecurity issues emanating from war, religion, header attacks, gender inequality, etc. Our organisation currently leverages on the gaps the government might not fill up by helping them in partnership to create an enabling stratosphere to improve the living conditions of rural dwellers with specialty on women, children, youth and physically challenged persons

AREAS OF SPECIALISATION
Food security via agriculture and agro-based industries
Environmental security:Sanitation,Water and environmental health
Providing Health Security e.g provision and adequate training of healthworkers
Providing Educational security e.g setting up scholarship funds
Funding the enforcement of human rights
Training and empowerment of Victimised citizens e.g provision of skill acquisition centers, soft loans for trading and agriculture etc
